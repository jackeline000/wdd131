Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Lilou website is to introduce a clean skincare brand that helps users find gentle, effective products made with safe ingredients. The quiz will use JavaScript to make it interactive and dynamic. As users answer questions, the quiz will show personalized skincare suggestions at the end based on their answers.

Audience

The intended audience is mostly women and teens who care about what they put on their skin. They are looking for safe, minimal, and clean skincare solutions that are easy to understand and feel good to use..
